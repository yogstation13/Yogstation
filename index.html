    • generic module - The base module type, which holds info about each ability.
      • destructive - Modules causing destruction
        • Blackout - Blackout: Overloads a random number of lights across the station. Three uses.
        • Destroy RCDs - Destroy RCDs: Detonates all non-cyborg RCDs on the station.
        • Hostile Station Lockdown - Hostile Station Lockdown: Locks, bolts, and electrifies every airlock on the station. After 90 seconds, the doors reset.
        • Doomsday Device - Doomsday Device: Starts the self-destruct timer. It can only be stopped by killing the AI completely.
        • Machine Overload - Overload Machine: Allows the AI to overload a machine, detonating it after a delay. Two uses per purchase.
        • Machine Override - Override Machine: Allows the AI to override a machine, animating it into an angry, living version of itself.
      • upgrade - Modules that are improving AI abilities and assets
        • Enhanced Surveillance - Enhanced Surveillance: Enables AI to hear conversations going on near its active vision.
        • Unlock Mech Domination - Unlock Mech Domination: Unlocks the ability to dominate mechs. Big shocker, right?
        • Upgrade Camera Network - Upgrade Camera Network: EMP-proofs all cameras, in addition to giving them X-ray vision.
        • AI Turret Upgrade - AI Turret Upgrade: Increases the health and damage of all turrets.
      • utility - Modules with stealthy and utility uses
        • Air Alarm Safety Override - Air Alarm Safety Override: Unlocks the ability to enable flooding on all air alarms.
        • Thermal Sensor Override - Thermal Sensor Override: Unlocks the ability to disable all fire alarms from doing their job.
        • Disable Emergency Lights - Disable Emergency Lights
        • Robotic Factory (Removes Shunting) - Robotic Factory: Places a large machine that converts humans that go through it into cyborgs. Unlocking this ability removes shunting.
        • Reactivate Camera Network - Reactivate Camera Network: Reactivates up to 30 cameras across the station.
